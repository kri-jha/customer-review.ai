Review AI

## Ecommerce Review Intelligence Dashboard

ReviewPulse AI is a wireframe dashboard designed for Ecommerce Brand Owners who receive thousands of customer reviews every month across multiple platforms such as Amazon, Flipkart, Shopify, Myntra, and others.

The goal of this solution is to bring all customer reviews into one centralized dashboard and convert unstructured feedback into clear, actionable business insights.

## Problem Statement

Ecommerce brands receive a large number of reviews across different online platforms.

Manually reading and managing these reviews is difficult because:

* Reviews are scattered across multiple platforms
* Important negative reviews may be missed
* Repeated customer complaints are difficult to identify
* Product performance is difficult to compare
* Support teams may not respond to customers on time
* Brand Owners cannot easily understand why customers are satisfied or dissatisfied

## Proposed Solution

ReviewPulse AI provides a centralized review management and customer feedback intelligence platform.

* Monitor reviews from multiple Ecommerce platforms
* Identify positive, neutral, and negative sentiment
* Detect recurring customer complaints
* Track product-level performance
* Highlight urgent and critical reviews
* Generate AI-assisted customer responses
* View actionable recommendations
* Monitor platform integration and review activity

## Key Features

### 1. Centralized Review Dashboard

All reviews from different Ecommerce platforms are displayed in one place.

### 2. Sentiment Analysis

Reviews are categorized as:

* Positive
* Neutral
* Negative

This helps the Brand Owner quickly understand overall customer satisfaction.

### 3. Product Performance Monitoring

The dashboard compares products using:

* Total reviews
* Average rating
* Positive sentiment
* Negative reviews
* Performance trends
* Product status

### 4. Top Customer Issues

The system identifies frequently reported problems such as:

* Delivery delays
* Damaged packaging
* Product quality issues
* Size or fit problems
* Refund delays

### 5. Urgent Review Detection

Critical reviews are highlighted based on:

* Low ratings
* Negative sentiment
* Product defects
* Refund complaints
* Delivery issues

### 6. AI Reply Suggestions

The dashboard provides AI-generated response suggestions in different tones:

* Empathetic
* Professional
* Friendly

Users can edit, copy, regenerate, or send the suggested response.

### 7. AI Recommended Actions

The system suggests actions such as:

* Investigating repeated packaging complaints
* Contacting delivery partners
* Updating product descriptions
* Prioritizing critical customer reviews
* Sharing positive feedback with the marketing team

### 8. Platform Integrations

The dashboard displays the connection and synchronization status of platforms such as:

* Amazon
* Flipkart
* Shopify
* Myntra

## How to Run the Project

1. Download or extract the project folder.
2. Open the folder.
3. Locate the `index.html` file.
4. Double-click `index.html`.
5. The dashboard will open in your default browser.

No installation or server setup is required.
